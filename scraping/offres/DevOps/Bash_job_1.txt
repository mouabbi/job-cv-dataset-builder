Key Responsibilities:
- Artifactory Administration & Maintenance - Configure, upgrade, and maintain JFrog Artifactory repositories (local, remote, and virtual), enforce access control policies, optimize storage, monitor performance, and troubleshoot artifact resolution issues in CI/CD workflows.
- Jenkins Pipeline Management & Upgrades - Design, develop, and maintain scalable Jenkins pipelines, integrate with Artifactory, optimize job execution, manage plugins, perform version upgrades, and implement secure credential handling for automation.
- Linux System Administration & Monitoring - Manage, upgrade, and optimize Linux-based CI/CD environments, automate system tasks using Bash/Ansible, monitor system health, and troubleshoot disk, process, and log-related issues to ensure high availability.
- Networking & Troubleshooting - Diagnose, monitor, and resolve network-related issues affecting CI/CD pipelines, debug DNS, firewall, proxy, and SSL/TLS problems, and use tools like tcpdump, curl, and netstat for analysis and proactive maintenance.
- Monitoring, Observability & Upgrades (Splunk, Dynatrace, CI/CD Health) - Administer, upgrade, and maintain Splunk for log management, create dashboards and alerts for system performance, integrate
- Dynatrace for real-time application monitoring, and ensure end-to-end observability for Jenkins, Artifactory, and infrastructure components.
Qualifications & Skills:
- Hands-on experience with JFrog Artifactory administration and repository management.
- Strong expertise in Jenkins pipeline development, maintenance, and upgrades.
- Proficiency in Linux system administration, scripting (Bash, Python), and automation (Ansible).
- Solid understanding of networking concepts, troubleshooting, and security protocols.
- Experience with monitoring tools (Splunk, Dynatrace, Prometheus, Grafana) for CI/CD observability.
- Knowledge of containerization (Docker, Kubernetes) and cloud-based CI/CD environments is a plus.
- Bachelor's degree in computer science, Engineering, Information Systems, or a related STEM field
- Overall 6 years of experience in GHES administration, with deep expertise in GitHub Actions, self-hosted runners, and microservices architecture.
- Strong scripting, API integration, and automation skills (GitHub REST/GraphQL APIs, GitHub CLI, Terraform, Ansible, Kubernetes).
