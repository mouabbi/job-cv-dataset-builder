Title: Infrastructure as Code Engineer
        Company: Western Alliance Bank
        Location: Phoenix, Maricopa County
        
        Description:
        Job Title:
Infrastructure as Code Engineer
Location:
Block 23
What you'll do:
This position requires experience creating and managing Infrastructure as Code and operational automation. You will engage directly with stakeholders to include Application and IT teams to identify automation opportunities and mapping out their software stacks and deployment processes. The ideal candidate will have experience with IaC development environments to include Azure Devops, Terraform, Ansible, and the Ansible Automation Platform, packer, python and other development languages. You will be expected to create and enhance our SDLC process, look for ways to improve our organization's maturity. Following Microsoft's best practices, work in our Git based SDLC workflows utilizing DevOps management platforms such as Azure DevOps, Gitlab, or Github.
Responsibilities:
Develop deployments of cloud resources using terraform
Design and implement a Golden Image baseline and revision process
Automate environment buildout in Azure
Automate disaster recovery environment deployments in Azure with terraform.
Customize environment buildout for specific applications
Design, build and maintain code repositories, processes, and procedures for specific application deployments
Liaison between IT and Application teams to identify automation opportunities.
Manage Ansible Linux Hosts, and Azure Marketplace deployment of Ansible Automation Platform.
What you'll need:
Qualifications:
5-10 years of related experience in IT automation and scripting.
Be proficient in Windows Ecosystem (Azure, O365, AD)
Have experience developing and testing scripts
Self motivated and able to work with teams in different locations
Bachelor's degree in computer science, software engineering, or a related study, or equivalent experience
Experience working in a bank environment providing infrastructure services to end users and/or development teams
Understanding and experience in designing & implementing modern solutions related to infrastructure while following Microsoft best practices.
Experience working with network, server, storage, application teams and product owners to develop automation.
Understanding of strategic, new, and emerging technology trends
Good written and verbal communication skills and can communicate with Executive Level stakeholders as well as Engineering teams.
Ability to build trust and respect as a thoughtful leader who can influence and persuade business and IT leaders and development teams.
Objective mindset that can think 'enterprise first' and remain unbiased toward any specific technology or vendor choice, with decisions made based on data, analysis, & POC results.
Ability to work effectively in a team environment.
Experience operating within a monitored and regulated environment, i.e., CIS compliant.
