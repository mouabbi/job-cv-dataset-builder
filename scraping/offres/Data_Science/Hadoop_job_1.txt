        A client of Innova Solutions is hiring for a *Hadoop Administrator**.*
*Position type: Full time - Contract *
*Duration: 24+ Months*
*Location:** San Antonio, TX*
As a *Hadoop Administrator, *you will:
* Be responsible for the care, maintenance, administration, and reliability of the Hadoop ecosystem.
* Ensure system security, stability, reliability, capacity planning, recoverability (protecting business data) and performance.
* Provide new system and data management solution delivery to meet the growing and evolving data demands of the enterprise.
*The ideal candidate will have: *
* Bachelor's degree in information systems, Engineering, Computer Science, or related field from an accredited university.
* Hands on experience with Hadoop administration in Linux and virtual environments.
* Experience in installing & managing distributions of Hadoop (Cloudera).
* Expert knowledge and hands-on experience in Hadoop ecosystem components; including HDFS, Yarn, Hive, LLAP, Druid, Impala, Spark, Kafka, HBase, Cloudera Work Bench, etc.
* Thorough knowledge of Hadoop overall architecture.
* Experience in using and troubleshooting Open-Source technologies including configuration management and deployment.
* Data Lake and Data Warehousing design and development.
* Experience reviewing existing DB and Hadoop infrastructure and determining areas of improvement.
* Scheduling and taking backups for Hadoop ecosystem.
* Good hands-on scripting experience in a Linux environment.
* Experience in project management concepts, tools (MS Project) and techniques.
