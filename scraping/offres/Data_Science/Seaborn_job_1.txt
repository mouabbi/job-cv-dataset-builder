Who We Are:
XSITE LLC is a dynamic Department of Defense (DoD) contractor specializing in advanced software information systems, systems engineering, and modeling and simulation support. We are Service-Disabled Veteran-Owned company headquartered in San Diego. We provide full spectrum systems and systems-of-systems engineering and integration services and solutions for space, C5ISR, cyber and enterprise information systems.
We offer an exciting opportunity for growth in a collaborative, cutting-edge and supportive environment where our engineers tackle complex, mission-critical challenges. As a small business, we offer a unique opportunity for individuals to gain hands-on experience in a dynamic and highly technical environment.
Position Overview:
XSITE is seeking a highly motivated and detail-oriented AI/ML Engineer to join our San Diego team full-time. This role offers the opportunity to support the Department of Defense by developing AI and ML tools, along with working to create tools for internal business development. These projects will follow an agile methodology by organizing work into “sprints”, with the scope for contract work decided by the Government, in order to adjust to changing circumstances or to capitalize on new opportunities discovered during the project.
Key Responsibilities:
Design, develop, and implement AI/ML models for government and internal use
Work with large datasets to preprocess and clean data
Optimize and fine-tune AI/ML models for performance, accuracy, and scalability
Develop and maintain documentation, testing procedures, and monitoring strategies for deployed applications
Leverage DevSecOps for all software development
Work as part of a contractor team alongside a broader team of government and contract staff
Stay up-to-date with the latest AI/ML trends, tools, and best practices
Performs other related duties as assigned by management
