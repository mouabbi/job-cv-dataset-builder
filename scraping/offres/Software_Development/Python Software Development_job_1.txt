Title: Python Software Developer
        Company: ASM Research, An Accenture Federal Services Company
        Location: Fort George G Meade, Anne Arundel County
        
        Description:
        - Transition the functionality of the PostGIS database and the functionality of the Jupyter Notebook based on customer direction.
- Develop prototypes answering new questions as well as providing better answers to existing questions based on customer direction.
- Develop a series of user facing query forms to leverage stored data to enable analysts to answer geospatial questions.
- Provide development support to analytic front ends to verify conformity and performance to proposed GUI designs.
- Provide operational maintenance and support for Customized/Do It Yourself analytics.
- Provide subject matter expertise for metric generation, usage, and applications.
Minimum Qualifications
Bachelor's degree plus 5-years of relevant experience or Master's degree plus 3-years of relevant experience. An Associate's degree plus 7-years of relevant experience or high school diploma/GED plus 9-years of relevant experience may be considered for individuals with in-depth experience that is clearly related to the position
Computer Science (CS) degree or related field
TS/SCI clearance with FS polygraph
Other Job Specific Skills
- Strong Python skills using PySpark, developing software in Red Hat Linux (versions 3 - 5+).
- Experience with Jupyter Notebooks.
- Experience with Machine Learning.
- Experience with GHOSTMACHINE analytic development.
- Familiarity with Query Time Analytics and DX.
- Familiarity with Redshift.
- Familiarity with Pandas.
- Familiarity with PostgreSQL.
- Strong problem solving, logic, and analytic skills.
