Position Overview:
We are hiring a Part-time Security Officer for Black Hills Corp at 7001 Mount Rushmore Road in Rapid City. You will secure the premises and personnel by conducting frequent patrols, monitoring surveillance equipment, performing building inspections, guarding entry points, and verifying visitors. The role requires attention to detail, physical stamina, and a strong commitment to safety and security.
Job Details:
• Pay Rate: $19.02 / hour
• Schedule: Morning Shift Available / 8:00 am – 4:00 pm / Saturday - Sunday.
• Duties: Foot Patrol and Access Control.
Qualifications:
• Experience: Graduate of Law Enforcement, Military, or Police Background (REQUIRED)
• Skills: Comfortable using technology devices
• Physical Requirements: Must be able to walk, stand for long periods, and work in all weather elements.
Join our esteemed team and honor your commitment to excellence. Explore a position where your contributions are recognized and valued.
Responsibilities:
- Provide customer service to our clients by carrying out safety and security procedures, site-specific policies and when appropriate, emergency response activities
- Respond to incidents and critical situations in a calm, problem solving manner
- Conduct regular and random patrols around the business and perimeter. Working environments and conditions may vary by client site.
