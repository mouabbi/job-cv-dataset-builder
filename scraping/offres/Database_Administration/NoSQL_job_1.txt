 Description & Requirements
Maximus is seeking a skilled Database Administrator to support vital systems in the defense of our Homeland. In this role, you will be involved in ensuring databases are properly managed, maintained, and optimized to meet business needs.
This position is an onsite role, 5 days a week and requires a secret clearance.
Maximus TCS (Technology and Consulting Services) Internal Job Profile Code: TCS082, T4, Band 7
Job-Specific Essential Duties and Responsibilities:
- Strong knowledge and hands-on experience with Database Management Systems (DBMS) such as SQL-based systems and NoSQL databases
- Understanding of database architecture, schemas, and normalization
- Familiarity with database specific programming languages (PL/SQL for Oracle, T-SQL for SQL Server)
- Ability to design, implement, and maintain database structures (e.g., tables, indexes, triggers, stored procedures)
- Experience in database backup strategies, disaster recovery planning, and execution
- Develop and maintain database backup and recovery strategies to ensure data availability and integrity
- Knowledge of optimizing database performance by tuning queries, indexing, and managing database configurations
- Experience using performance monitoring tools
- Administer and maintain multiple Server database environments, including installation, configuration, and patch management
- Create and maintain documentation related to database configurations, procedures, and troubleshooting guides
Job-Specific Minimum Requirements:
- 7+ years of work related experience required.
- A High School diploma or GED is required.
- Active Secret clearance is required.
- Due to federal requirements, only US Citizens can be considered. Candidates with dual citizenship cannot be considered.
- This is an on-site position at a government facility.
- Requires presence on-site five days per week in Colorado Springs, CO.
- Candidates must reside within a commutable distance.
- Participation in a rotational on-call schedule for emergency page-outs is required.
