Description
We are seeking an experienced Wireless Network Engineer to join our team and provide critical support to improve the stability and reliability of our wireless infrastructure. The wireless network is currently facing significant challenges, and we need a wireless guru to help troubleshoot and optimize the environment. This role will involve logical troubleshooting, network analysis, and ensuring seamless wireless performance across a hospital environment.
Responsibilities:
- Provide expert-level support for wireless network stability and performance.
- Troubleshoot and resolve wireless connectivity issues across a large-scale network.
- Support the transition from Cisco to Juniper Mist 4000 series with a focus on seamless deployment.
- Optimize and manage the performance of approximately 2000 Cisco 9000 APs.
- Collaborate closely with internal teams and stakeholders to implement wireless solutions.
- Provide proactive recommendations for network improvements.
- Serve as a subject matter expert for wireless network design and architecture.
Must-Have Qualifications:
- Expertise with Juniper Mist 4000 series for wireless deployment and management.
- Strong troubleshooting and problem-solving skills, particularly in complex wireless environments.
Nice to Have (Bonus):
- Experience with Clearpass 802.1x and/or Cisco ISE for network authentication and security.
Additional Details:
- This role is primarily focused on logical deployment and troubleshooting rather than physical installations.
